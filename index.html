Gmail Send As Guideline

How to configure Gmail to send as another email address

Add Send As Address Verify & Use

Step 1: Add a "Send As" Address in Gmail

1.1 Open Gmail Settings

Click the gear icon in the top right

1.2 Select See all settings

Navigate to the Accounts and Import tab.

1.3 Go to Accounts and Import
1.4 Add Another Email Address

Under Send mail as, click Add another email address.

Enter the name and email address you want to send as, then click Next Step.

Step 2: Verify and Use the Send As Address

2.1 Confirm Verification

Click "Send Verification"

2.2 Confirm Verification from another account

Gmail will send a verification message to the added email address. Check your inbox, click the link and "Confirm"

2.3 Verification

Back to "Accounts and Import" click on verify

Click on "Save Changes"

Congratulations! You can now send emails from Gmail using the "Send as" Feature
